About

The Library Branch rescues used books from local library book stores and redistributes them throughout the community through scheduled book drops, locally sponsored Book Adoption Branches, and stocking existing Little Free Libraries.

What started as trying to find a new home for a couple extra books grew into an initiative to bring neighbors together through a love of reading.

Our goal for 2026 is to facilitate the redistribution of 500 books!
